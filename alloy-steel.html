Alloy Steel Products

High-Grade Alloy Steel in Multiple Grades and Forms

About Alloy Steel

Alloy steel is steel that is alloyed with a variety of elements in total amounts between 1.0% and 50% by weight to improve its mechanical properties. We stock a comprehensive range of alloy steel grades suitable for various industrial applications including automotive, manufacturing, tool making, and engineering sectors.

Our alloy steel products are sourced from reputed manufacturers and come with proper mill test certificates ensuring quality and traceability. Available in round bars, square bars, flat bars, and custom sizes as per your requirements.

Available Grades

Comprehensive Range of Alloy Steel Grades

EN19 (42CrMo4)

High tensile alloy steel with excellent strength and toughness properties.

  • Diameter: 10mm to 150mm
  • Finish: Hot Rolled / Bright
  • Applications: Gears, Shafts, Axles
  • Hardness: 28-32 HRC (Hardened)

EN24 (817M40)

Nickel chromium molybdenum steel with high tensile strength.

  • Diameter: 10mm to 150mm
  • Finish: Black / Bright
  • Applications: Crankshafts, Gears
  • Hardness: 30-35 HRC (Hardened)

EN31 (SAE 52100)

High carbon chromium bearing steel with excellent wear resistance.

  • Diameter: 10mm to 100mm
  • Finish: Black / Bright
  • Applications: Bearings, Dies, Rollers
  • Hardness: 60-65 HRC (Hardened)

EN8 (080M40)

Medium carbon steel with good machinability and tensile strength.

  • Diameter: 10mm to 300mm
  • Finish: Black / Bright
  • Applications: Shafts, Axles, Gears
  • Hardness: 20-25 HRC (Normalized)

SAE 4140

Chromium molybdenum alloy steel with high fatigue strength.

  • Diameter: 10mm to 200mm
  • Finish: Hot Rolled / Bright
  • Applications: Gears, Shafts, Bolts
  • Hardness: 25-30 HRC (Normalized)

D2 Tool Steel

High carbon, high chromium tool steel with excellent wear resistance.

  • Diameter: 10mm to 100mm
  • Finish: Black / Bright
  • Applications: Dies, Punches, Tools
  • Hardness: 58-62 HRC (Hardened)
