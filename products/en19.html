EN19 Alloy Steel

EN19 is a high-quality alloy steel known for its exceptional tensile strength and toughness. Widely used in manufacturing gears, shafts, and heavy-duty components requiring superior mechanical properties.

Technical Specifications

Material Grade EN19 (709M40)
Tensile Strength 850-1000 N/mm²
Yield Strength 680 N/mm² (min)
Hardness 248-302 HB
Available Forms Round Bars, Flat Bars, Plates
Standard Sizes 10mm to 500mm diameter

Key Features & Applications

  • High tensile strength and toughness
  • Excellent wear resistance
  • Good machinability in annealed condition
  • Ideal for gears, shafts, and axles
  • Suitable for heavy-duty applications
  • Used in automotive and engineering industries
  • Can be heat treated for enhanced properties
