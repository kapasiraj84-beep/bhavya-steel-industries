Excellence in Steel Distribution

Bhavya Steel Industries is a premier stockist and supplier of high-quality alloy steel and mild steel products based in Naroda, Ahmedabad. With a commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction, we serve diverse industries including construction, manufacturing, automotive, and engineering sectors across Gujarat.

Our extensive inventory includes premium alloy steel grades (EN19, EN24, EN31, EN8, EN9, SAE 4140, D2, D3, F11), mild steel products in various forms (round bars, square bars, flat bars, hexagon bars), bright bars with superior finish, metal strips, stainless steel plates, and a comprehensive range of industrial tools and hardware.

Strategically located near NH-8 in Naroda, we ensure timely delivery and competitive pricing while maintaining the highest quality standards. Our experienced team provides expert technical support to help you select the right materials for your specific applications.
