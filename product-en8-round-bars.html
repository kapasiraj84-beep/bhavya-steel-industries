EN8 Round Bars

EN8 (080M40) is a medium carbon steel grade offering excellent machinability and good tensile strength. Widely used in general engineering applications, EN8 round bars are ideal for manufacturing shafts, axles, gears, and other mechanical components requiring reliable strength and durability.

Applications & Uses

EN8 round bars are extensively used across various industries due to their excellent mechanical properties and machinability:

  • Automotive Industry: Crankshafts, axles, gears, connecting rods, and transmission components
  • General Engineering: Shafts, spindles, studs, bolts, and general-purpose machinery parts
  • Manufacturing: Machine tool components, jigs, fixtures, and tooling applications
  • Construction Equipment: Heavy-duty pins, bushings, and structural components
  • Agricultural Machinery: Tractor parts, harvester components, and farm equipment
  • Industrial Equipment: Pump shafts, conveyor components, and material handling systems

Key Features & Benefits

  • Excellent Machinability: Easy to machine, drill, and turn with standard tooling
  • Good Tensile Strength: Provides reliable strength for general engineering applications
  • Heat Treatable: Can be hardened and tempered to achieve desired mechanical properties
